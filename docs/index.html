Manifestation in the virtual World

TheSystem - Artificial Museum is the connection between physical, digital, and imaginary worlds, transforming analog art into digital experiences.

These "augmented" artworks form the foundation of an artificial museum, allowing artists to bridge the chasm between their art and the evolving digitized world around them.

The Museum is connected to the real world through the web-based augmented reality application you are currently visiting, we hope you will enjoy your stay.

THESYSTEM Privacy Notice.

We promise to never track, analyze, or save any data about you on a computer that you do not control.

Local Visual data

All browser features that collect data (device orientation, 3d-map of your surroundings, camera video streams) DO NOT and NEVER WILL transmit this data.

Gelocation

The geolocation information we collect never gets transmitted anywhere.

Session storage

We save some data in your browser (your current location and which page you opened), those get deleted as soon as you close the browser tab.

Hosting provider

At the moment, this homepage is hosted on github, who promise that they do not track users using unique identifiers either. For more information, please refer to the github privacy disclaimer

Correlation

We never correlate the images or objects you view.
